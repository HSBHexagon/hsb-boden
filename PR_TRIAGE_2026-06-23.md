- **Regeln:** PR-Pflicht (`pull_request`, 1 Approval), `required_status_checks` **strict** mit 8 Kontexten: `validate`, `build-and-test`, `Dependency Review`, `Secret Scanning`, `lighthouse`, `deploy`, `Analyze (javascript-typescript)`, `Analyze (actions)`; `deletion` + `non_fast_forward` blockiert.
